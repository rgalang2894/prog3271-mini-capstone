Welcome to our plant management app where you manage and find and manage all of your botany needs. Here you can manage and add your houseplants, get plant identification from our plant database, and get reminders for specific treatments for any of your plants. You can also find plant stores around you for any of your plant needs e.g. new plants, seeds, special equipment, etc.

Our frontend will contain a homepage containing plants that the user has added with total CRUD options, notification tab related to reminders the user has set, and a store tab containing local plant stores. We will also have a catalog of plants you can favorite and see details on for any future interest and/or plant identification.
